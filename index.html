Σκάλα Σωτήρος · The island

Skala Sotiros, on the emerald isle

Thassos is the greenest of the Greek islands — pine forest tumbling straight down to turquoise water, white-marble quarries the Romans prized, and a coastline of easy, family-friendly beaches.

Skala Sotiros sits on the calm west coast: a relaxed seaside village with tavernas, a sand-and-pebble beach, and sunsets over the water. Ferries reach the island from Keramoti and Kavala on the mainland.
