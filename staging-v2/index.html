SKALA SOTIROS · THE WEST COAST

A real village, and the perfect base to tour the island.

Skala Sotiros sits on the calm, west-facing coast of Thassos — the quiet side, where the water is shallow and easy for children and the evenings stay peaceful. A working Greek village, not a resort strip: a short walk to the beach, a few tavernas, and sunsets off the balcony.

Because it's mid-island, the whole of Thassos is an easy day-drive in either direction — Golden Beach, Aliki, the marble villages and the mountain tavernas. Most guests arrive by car: down through the northern borders, then the short Keramoti–Thassos ferry (or Kavala–Skala Prinos), and a 15–20 minute coastal drive to the door.
